Chapter I

The Timer

One button. Press start, read, press stop. Foxlit counts the minutes so you don't have to think about it — no goals to configure, nothing to set up before your first page.

Chapter II

The Streak

Every day you read, the flame grows. Miss a day, and it's honest about that too — no forgiving asterisks, just a simple, visible record of the habit you're actually building.
